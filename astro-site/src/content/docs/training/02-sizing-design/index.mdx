## Learning Objectives

By the end of this module, you will be able to:

- **Evaluate customer requirements** and recommend an appropriate VergeOS topology (HCI, UCI, or hybrid)
- **Size hardware** for HCI and UCI deployments based on workload profiles
- **Use reference architectures** as starting points for customer designs
- **Create a bill of materials** for a VergeOS deployment proposal

## Prerequisites

- Completion of [Module 1: Architecture Fundamentals](/training/01-architecture/) -- you should understand HCI vs UCI trade-offs, node types, and cluster concepts

## Estimated Time

**2 hours** (1 hour reading + 1 hour lab)
